Can silence be more than the absence of sound? In a society where people move around spaces without any pause, Shiopi highlights the act of listening using the blank sonic space that silence offers to unfold its full potential

Shiopi presents itself as a Sound_Installation/Sound_Object that explores memories of natural environments and the sonification of the invisible sourroundings, with silence as its catalytic element

Shiopi continuously searches for a quiet environment that enables focused listening. Silence is understood as a space open to the listening process. The sound material of Shiopi transforms itself according to the possibilities of immersing oneself in the listening process.

When the space is quiet, silence casts a spell, creating unreal and magical soundscapes that fuses field recordings of natural environments with electronic sounds produced through the sonification of invisible data in real time (UV radiation, ambient light, temperature and humidity)

On the contrary, when the listening process is broken, only the nature sounds remains, closing the gateaway to perceiving the unseen.

There is a hidden third layer of sound that only emerges when the noise level in the space is overwhelming. This sound material is disruptive in its essence and functions as a final warning to restore quietness. This sound layer vibrates with a pink noise filtered according to the fundamental frequency of the space and modulated with a sinusoidal wave

**shiopi does not react to sudden and accidental noises but instead measures the average silence level over approximately the last 11.6 seconds**

SHIOPI was premiered at TTTArts 2025 in Ljubljana

Can we hear all the vibrations that surround us? Our human ear can perceive a limited spectrum of frecruencies, besides, some stimuli do not manifest as an acoustic wave. To expand the meaning of soundscape, shiopi transform unheard environmental data into electronic sounds,

This data controls four different synths and their internal patterns -texture, rhythm, frecuencies,etc. Most of the features are controlled in a subtle manner, using a complex internal mapping logic. However, the UV radition determinates, in a straight corelation, when the synths are activated and deactivated, creating two contrasting sound_atmospheres: brighter/magical vs dark/mystery

HOW DOES IT WORK? DIY+OPEN_SOURCES

ESP32 & SENSORS: ALS-PT19-UVLTR390-DHT22-BME280

PUREDATA: main patch -> receive&send info through OSC, maps the values, controls the level of silence and the sound layers

SUPERCOLLIDER: generates the electronic sounds
